Enterprise Server Operations Center

US Department of State

Denver, CO

In response to changing technology and declining capacity, the US Department of State (DOS) required a new operations and data center to support domestic and worldwide DOS information and technology processing. The 110,000-sf building maintains Tier 3 Uptime reliability and redundancy, and infrastructure to accommodate 12 megawatts of computing. Despite the electricity needed to run the computer, the innovative mechanical system is designed to be 70% more efficient than EPAct standards.

The new Enterprise Server Operations Center provides highly available, scalable, and redundant data center infrastructure that substantially reduces the Department’s risk and provides for future IT growth. It enables DOS to operate continuously and without interruption, by accommodating maintenance to all equipment without outages.
